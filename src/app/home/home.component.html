Our Story

Bee Way Honey Pollination is family owned and operated since 1993. We take pride in our products, ensuring only the best quality to our customers. All of our products are raw and natural. Our honey varieties include blueberry, clover, raspberry, blackberry, and wildflower and are all harvested in Oregon and Washington.
